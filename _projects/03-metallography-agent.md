Can agentic AI also be used for questions and applications in metallography that are usually very domain-specific and cannot always be reliably answered by a standard LLM?
That is exactly what we are examining in this project. The foundation for this consists of structured databases containing text-based knowledge, images, and preparation recipes. 
We are currently building these databases and using LLMs for literature mining and annotation to convert various data sources into uniform structures.
