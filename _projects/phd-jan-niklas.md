In this PhD project, we are investigating the use of machine learning to predict material properties. Rather than predicting a single global parameter, we are focusing on predicting a locally resolved parameter mapping. To achieve this, we are investigating image-to-image translation models that can predict property mappings directly from microstructure images. This approach is based on cross-scale microstructure characterization, the generation of experimental data through ex-situ and in-situ mechanical tests, and simulation-to-experiment pretraining to enrich the experimental data.
