This thesis, which we are co-supervising and which is primarily being carried out at Montan University Leoben, aims to optimize spark plasma sintering processes for ceramics.
During her exchange stay at our group, Mahima Haque is using machine learning models for the quantification of grain sizes and porosities.
The results are then used in a bayesian optimization framework for inverse process design and guided experimentation.
