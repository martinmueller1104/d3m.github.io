---
title: "D3M secures grant from Saarland University funding program"
date: 2026-05-29
---

As part of Saarland University’s internal research program “Anschub Finanzierung” which supports research projects aimed at preparing applications for third-party funding, we were able to secure funding to conduct research on “Correlation Between Multiphase Material Microstructures and Local Micromechanical Properties Using Artificial Intelligence”!

Stay tuned for updates on the project, which will begin in September 2026!
