title: "Visit at Max Planck Institute for Sustainable Materials"
date: 2026-06-25
---

On June 24, 2026, Martin Müller visited Prof. Dierk Raabe at the Max Planck Institute for Sustainable Materials in Düsseldorf. In addition to in-depth technical discussions with Prof. Raabe’s research groups (Circular Metallurgy and Alloy Design, AI in Materials Science) and a lab tour, Martin Müller gave a seminar presentation on the role of correlative microscopy in the context of ML-based microstructure analysis (title: “Advancing AI-Driven Microstructure Analysis through Correlative Microscopy Approaches”).
The discussion was incredibly engaging. We look forward to the follow-up, and we’d like to thank you for your hospitality!
